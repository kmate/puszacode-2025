# PuszaCode 2025 - Challenge Summary

## Overview
24 beginner-friendly programming challenges with smooth difficulty progression.

## Difficulty Levels

### **Days 1-6: Basics** (Warm-up)
Foundation concepts: functions, strings, numbers, conditionals

### **Days 7-12: Arrays & Strings** (Easy)
Working with collections and iteration

### **Days 13-18: Intermediate Concepts** (Medium)
Algorithms, transformations, and data manipulation

### **Days 19-24: Advanced** (Challenging)
Problem-solving, recursion, and classic algorithms
